Cinnamon Brewet

Cinnamon Brewet is a dish from 14th century France, it is a fancy way of saying its a pork roast with cinnamon.

Ingredients:

  • 4 lbs. Beef Roast
  • 2 cups Almonds, ground
  • 2 tsp. Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. Ginger
  • 1/2 tsp. Cloves
  • 2-4 cups Red Wine
  • 2-3 cups Beef stock

Preparation:

  1. Season beef with sprinkling of cinnamon and brown on all sides.
  2. Place into a roasting pan add red wine and beef stock and half of the rest of the spices.
  3. Braise in a 350Â°F oven for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until well done.
  4. Remove from oven; take beef from roasting pan, drain juices and separate fat.
  5. Place meat broth into a sauce pan and bring to a boil, add almonds and begin to thicken
  6. Taste, add additional spices as needed.
  7. Strain thickened sauce.
  8. Slice meat and serve with thickened sauce.
